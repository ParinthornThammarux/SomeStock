Type: Reversal Candlestick

‚úÖ How to Use:
Appears at the bottom of a downtrend

üß≠ Interpretation:
Bullish reversal signal when confirmed by a strong bullish candle after the hammer

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

‚úÖ How to Use:

üß≠ Interpretation:

# üìä 4. EMA Cross (Exponential Moving Average Crossover)
Type: Trend-following Indicator

‚úÖ How to Use:
Use two EMAs, such as:

Fast EMA: 9-day or 12-day

Slow EMA: 26-day or 50-day

üß≠ Interpretation:
Bullish Cross (Golden Cross): Fast EMA crosses above slow EMA ‚Üí Buy signal

Bearish Cross (Death Cross): Fast EMA crosses below slow EMA ‚Üí Sell signal

Works best in trending markets, not sideways markets

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
